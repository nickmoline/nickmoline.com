Nick Moline is a senior software engineer at Justia.com, a company in Mountain View, California dedicated to making the law free and available for everyone. At Justia Nick works on a variety of free legal information projects with the rest of the talented engineering team there.

Unfortunately he struggled with being overweight all his life. In January, 2011 enough was enough and Nick decided to start losing weight. He weighed in at 490.2 pounds. After 2 years of diet, exercise and surgery, Nick currently (as of April 7, 2013) weighs in at 309.5 pounds, 180.7 pounds lost. He still has a long way to go, but he's proud of his progress and looks forward to breaking the 300 pound barrier very soon.
